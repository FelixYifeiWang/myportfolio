God took 7 days to create the world. Now, you can create yours in minutes.

Initiative

Video games are one of the most popular content mediums worldwide, with over 3 billion gamers. Yet, unlike text, images, or videos, most gaming enthusiasts lack the tools or skills to create games themselves.

This project seeks to democratize game development. By leveraging generative AI and cloud gaming, we enable anyone to create fully functional games in minutes, eliminating technical barriers. Just as platforms like e-books, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube revolutionized content creation for text, images, and videos, we aim to bring the same decentralization to gaming.

Research & Ideation

My cofounders and I are passionate indie game developers, naturally surrounded by gamers. A common request we hear is from people sharing their game ideas. Some are artists, some are writers, and some are just gamers with vivid imaginations. We've recommended tools like Godot, RPG Maker, or Roblox to help them, but none fully met their needs. While these tools aren’t the ultimate solution, they’ve inspired us in shaping our approach.

After researching all lightweight game engines on the market and collectively conducting nearly 100 hours of interviews with people sharing this need, we created our user persona storyboard.

Prototyping

To validate the need, we built a minimum viable product on an emerging online social platform called Gather Town. Using its built-in multiplayer and visual features, we developed a plug-in that, with some manual effort, transformed a testing user's paragraph description into an interactive, game-like experience. We then distributed these experiences to other testing users and formed a small community. The result was a high satisfaction and recommendation rate among the test group.

Product Design & Development

Drawing inspiration from Midjourney, we built our user community on Discord, leveraging its adaptable APIs to create an efficient content distribution system. To ensure both quality and accessibility for our game content and engine, we developed an integrated website closely tied to our Discord community. Users can create games through the same link, and each game is assigned a unique URL that is automatically shared within the community.

The game experience and engine were built using Unity. Our core feature was a conversational interface, where an AI copilot guides the creator by asking questions and generating corresponding game assets based on their answers. We created a custom training dataset of around 30 images for characters and game maps, training separate image generation models. For dialogues and certain game mechanics, we utilized GPT-3.5. To kickstart the project, we collaborated with two game dev-focused YouTubers, which proved successful.

Outcome

• Within 3 months of beta testing, over 3,000 users were involved, with 2,000+ still on the waitlist.

• 3,200+ games were created, surpassing the number of games uploaded to Steam during the same period.

• Logged over 200,000 gameplay sessions.

• The most popular YouTube video about our project garnered over 200k views.

• Gained a deeper understanding of the potential and current limitations of AI in game creation, laying the groundwork for future improvements.
