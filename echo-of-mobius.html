Nobody wants their AI companions stay in a chat box.

Initiative

AI accompaniment is gradually becoming a part of more and more people's lives, and it has become a new content carrier. However, most AI companion apps on the market limit its potential to boring chat boxes.

As passionate gamers and early adopters of AI characters, my startup team launched this project to create an immersive game world where users can interact with AI characters in more fun and engaging ways. It features a user-generated content ecosystem that allows players to bring their imaginations to life while experiencing infinite content collectively created by the community.

Research & Ideation

Our team started with a ton of market research. By "a ton," I mean we tried almost every AI chatbot app out there. We also spent hours daily in Reddit communities, joining discussions and interviewing users about why they quit or stayed hooked. Then, we explored role-playing games to find the best gameplay modes.

From our research, we found a significant overlap between AI chatbot users and RPG players. Both groups consist primarily of creative and imaginative young people and teenagers who are willing to treat virtual characters as real beings. It is clear that they expect the experience to become more immersive and interactive. Some AI chatbot users are beginning to lose interest due to repetitive and simplistic interactions. Based on these insights, we developed this simple user persona.

Prototyping

Building a full game is a huge effort, so we wanted to make sure we were on the right track first. Instead of diving straight in, we created a simple Discord bot as our MVP. With it, users could create and chat with characters, take them on text adventures, battle others (purely random winners), and watch them evolve. Our testers loved the community, and it grew quickly and organically, validating the need for our project.

Product Development

With our MVP validating demand, we moved forward with building the game. From our market research, we identified turn-based battles and CRPG text-pic adventures as the best gameplay modes because of the broad audience and creative space, and are relatively simple to implement. We first developed a basic draft and recruited about 30 testers to help us refine visuals and balance gameplay mechanics.

On the technical side, the main challenge was integrating generative AI seamlessly into a production-level game. Our team of three 2D artists (including myself) created 130 virtual characters as a training dataset. We fine-tuned SDXL (later replaced with Flux) using LoRA techniques. To standardize the generated images, I developed a CV sequence algorithm that handled tasks like background removal, and adjustments to exposure, saturation, and size.

For character interactions, we used APIs from both OpenAI and Anthropic. GPT excelled at command interactions, while Claude generated more engaging narratives and dialogues. Each character was paired with a memory system designed by us to track player choices, which could influence the character’s development. To enhance immersion, we implemented fixed-perspective 3D scenes for the game’s visuals.

Growth & Operation

Our main growth strategy is collaborating with streamers. The game's creative freedom makes it easy for them to create engaging content, often leading to their highest-ever CCV. Many streamers promoted our game for free because of this. Additionally, the UGC ecosystem has driven organic growth, as players naturally share and expand the game themselves.

Impact

• Launched in late October, now with 2,000+ users and 300+ paying players, still growing rapidly. It has established a sustainable business model with an impressive 90% profit margin.

• Over 20,000 unique characters created, making it the game with the most playable characters worldwide.

• Achieved a second-week retention rate of 26% and an average session time of 30+ minutes.

• Over 30 streamers featured the game, reaching 50k+ views across platforms.

• It has become the game with the most playable characters in the world, showcasing the transformative potential of generative AI technology in revolutionizing video games.
